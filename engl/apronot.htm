(*) Both the classification of the document, considered as a project by the authors of the documental compilation from which we have taken it, and the date they attribute to it -04.13.1928- are, in our opinion, mistaken. The resolution became effective in January 1927 as Oscar Pérez Solís makes evident in his Letter to the Executive Committee of the Party.

(*) The first call to the V Congress was made for the summer of 1936, but it did not take place by then due to the fascist uprising. Later on, with the defeat of the Republic and the immediate outbreak of World War II and the subsequent scattering of cadres and militants through different countries, new difficulties appeared for its holding. However, after the liberation of France, the Leadership, each time more influenced by the revisionist trend, did nothing to carry out the calling of the V Congress -which could not be postponed any longer- in order to avoid giving an account of its serious responsibilities for the mistakes committed by the Party in the final stage of the war and afterwards.
